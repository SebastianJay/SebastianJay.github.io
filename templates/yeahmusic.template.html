Overview

Stay alive! And make some sweet tunes while doing so. There are a total of 49 loops that can be mixed for 75 = 16807 "songs." Note: Unity's web player canvas is not very responsive, so play this game on a machine that can fit the whole canvas.

Controls

  • Space or Up: Jump
  • Left/Right: Move laterally
  • Press Space to start/retry a game
